Welcome to Social Conclave

About the Event

Social Conclave is a one of a kind conference where delegates from across the country gather together to discuss the most contentious social issues, with the aim to find a sustainable solution.

We discuss various issues like: the safety of women, sanitation of urban areas, and cyber bullying; aiming at finding feasible solutions toward which we along with our team can take steps and bring about positive and impactful changes.

However, our goal doesn't stop there. Unlike other conferences, where the scope is limited to discussing issues, we at Social Conclave will also make sure that the solutions agreed upon at the conference will be implemented at ground level.

We understand that discussion of such critical issues requires expert guidance and hence we also have eminent speakers at the conference who get to the crux of the problems and help the students get a better understanding of the problems, and ultimately ending in providing more sustainable solutions.

Although Social Conclave might only have a span of three days, we aim to create an impact in society that will have a lasting effect. We hope to ignite a fire inside our delegates and birth India's next generation of change-makers.

We hope to inspire this generation with the idea that change is all about starting now. Every step taken in the right direction inspires someone around us. And together, we can create bigger changes.
